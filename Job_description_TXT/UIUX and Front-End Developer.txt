Internship Description
About Tapleto Host: Tapleto Host is a leading provider of hosting solutions specifically tailored for gamers and websites. We offer high-performance, reliable, and secure hosting services to our clients, enabling them to have seamless online experiences. As a growing company in the hosting industry, we are committed to delivering exceptional services to our customers while continuously innovating and improving our product offerings.

Job Description: We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated UI/UX and Front-End Developer to join our talented team. As a UI/UX and Front-End Developer at Tapleto Host, you will play a crucial role in designing and developing user-friendly interfaces that enhance our customers' experience. You will be responsible for creating visually appealing and intuitive user interfaces, ensuring optimal website performance, and collaborating closely with our design and development teams.

Responsibilities:

Design and develop responsive user interfaces for our hosting solutions, including websites and gamer-focused applications.
Collaborate with the design team to translate wireframes and mockups into engaging and visually appealing user interfaces.
Optimize web pages for maximum speed and scalability, ensuring cross-browser compatibility.
Implement front-end functionalities using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while adhering to best practices and industry standards.
Conduct UI/UX testing to gather user feedback and iterate on designs for continuous improvement.
Collaborate with back-end developers to integrate front-end components with server-side logic.
Stay up to date with industry trends, best practices, and emerging technologies to propose innovative solutions.


Internship Requirements
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Web Development, or a related field (or equivalent work experience).
Proven experience as a UI/UX and Front-End Developer, showcasing a strong portfolio of past projects.
Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and related frameworks/libraries (e.g., React, Angular, Vue.js).
Solid understanding of user-centered design principles, responsive design, and web accessibility standards.
Experience with UI/UX design tools (e.g., Sketch, Adobe XD, Figma) and prototyping tools (e.g., InVision, Marvel).
Familiarity with version control systems (e.g., Git) and agile development methodologies.
Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
Excellent communication skills and attention to detail.
Preferred Skills:

Experience in the hosting industry or a similar technology-related field.

Knowledge of back-end technologies and frameworks (e.g., Node.js, Ruby on Rails).

Understanding of SEO principles and best practices.

Familiarity with graphic design principles and tools (e.g., Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator).

How to Apply: If you are passionate about UI/UX design and front-end development, and you're excited to contribute to the success of a growing hosting company focused on gamers and websites, we would love to hear from you. Please submit your resume, portfolio, and cover letter highlighting your relevant experience to our email address. Include any links to your online profiles (GitHub, Dribbble, Behance, etc.) if available.
